computer

  • What is a computer? What is another name for computer? The root of the word computer is the word Compute, which means calculating, of course, calculating with a machine. In fact, computer means counter and calculator, and in the beginning, the purpose of computer invention was this. The computer is a programmable machine that has affected all of our lives in different ways. These days, you can hardly find a business that is not directly or indirectly related to computers. To put it simply, a computer is a device for processing raw data. A computer is a device that takes raw information and converts it into processed and understandable information. In simple terms, for example, when you are typing a text, the information is sent to the computer by the keyboard and the result is displayed to us through the monitor. Of course, don't forget that new computers have high processing capabilities and can perform very high math calculations in a few thousandths of a second.

loptop

  • Laptops are small, light and portable personal computers that are used in contrast to desktop computers. The weight of a laptop is usually between 1 and 7 kilos and it will be able to continue working with a battery. Laptops generally have the same components used in a desktop computer, except that the weight and size have been reduced as much as possible. The first laptops, or portable computers, called Dynabooks, were implemented in 1970 by Ellen Kay and were as small as a notebook; But the honor of making the original laptop belongs to the great company IBM, which was able to introduce the laptop to the world market in 1975.

phone

  • Cell phone, mobile or phone, is a device for sending and receiving sound and video through bts in a wide geographic area. Mobile phone, or cell phone, is a device that is used to connect to the mobile phone network. The first wireless phone was made by Martin Cooper in 1973 with a weight of nearly 200 grams. Mobile phones have different generations and are produced and sold by different manufacturers. Some of the major mobile phone manufacturers in the world include: Nokia, Samsung, Huawei, LG, Sony, Apple...
